Our firm provides family litigation services in all levels of Ontario court as well as administrative tribunals respecting pensions and benefits. Aside from litigation, we represent clients with amicable and negotiated separations and divorces. We also provide planning advice and services relating to wills, estates, powers of attorney and cohabitation agreements.
